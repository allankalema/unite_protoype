Why UNITE eLearn

UNITE eLearn is an AI-enhanced digital learning platform built for teacher professional development. It combines self-paced modules, assessments, and certification with an AI tutor that supports understanding at lesson level.

For Teachers

  • Enroll in practical training courses
  • Track progress and complete lesson pathways
  • Take quizzes and unlock certificates
  • Ask AI tutor for simplified explanations

For Staff

  • Create and manage content quickly
  • Monitor enrollments and completions
  • Track AI tutor usage for support insights
  • Present clear demo metrics to stakeholders
